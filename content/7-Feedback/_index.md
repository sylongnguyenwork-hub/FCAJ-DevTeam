> Here you can freely share your personal feedback regarding your experiences while participating in the First Cloud Journey program, helping the FCJ team improve and address existing gaps based on the following categories:

### General Assessment

**1. Working Environment**  
The workplace environment is consistently clean, friendly, and open. Team members always proactively support one another in their daily tasks.

**2. Support from Mentors / Admin Team**  
Mentors provide highly detailed guidance, explaining concepts clearly whenever I encounter difficulties, and continuously encourage me to ask questions. The admin team offers excellent support with administrative procedures and documentation, creating the best possible conditions for me to work smoothly.

**3. Alignment Between Assigned Tasks and Academic Major**  
The initial tasks assigned were quite new compared to my academic major. However, this did not pose an issue as fellow interns and mentors were always ready to support each other, enabling me to explore and acquire a wealth of new knowledge.

**4. Opportunities for Learning & Skill Development**  
I have acquired numerous new skills, including utilizing project management tools, effective teamwork capabilities, and professional workplace communication.

**5. Corporate Culture & Team Spirit**  
Everyone treats each other like family, yet maintains a high level of professionalism and seriousness when it comes to work execution.

**6. Policies & Benefits for Interns**  
The company offers flexible time arrangements when necessary. Additionally, being granted the opportunity to attend internal training sessions is a significant advantage.

### Additional Questions

**1. What are you most satisfied with during your internship?**  
What I am most satisfied with during my internship is the flexible working hours, which perfectly accommodate students like me who balance studying with an internship.

**2. What do you think the company needs to improve for future interns?**  
If possible, the company should provide more sessions and physical spaces to encourage and facilitate interns to come to the office more frequently.

**3. If recommending to friends, would you advise them to intern here? Why?**  
Yes, absolutely. This is an excellent internship environment to foster personal growth, improve self-directed learning skills, and significantly enhance their future CVs.
